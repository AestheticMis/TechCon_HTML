History

TechCon began in 2010 with the mission of connecting innovators and thought leaders across the global tech community. Over the years, it has grown into a premier event attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Mission

TechCon aims to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of technology leaders through meaningful discussion, networking, and collaboration.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has featured keynote addresses by industry icons:

  • Ada Lovelace – Pioneer of computer programming and an advocate for women in STEM.

  • Alan Turing – Known for foundational work in artificial intelligence and computing.
